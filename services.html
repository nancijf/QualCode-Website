Services


QualCode works seamlessly within your organization to give you the help you need. Our goal is eliminating your coding and billing headaches and helping you maintain steady revenue streams.

Our services include the following:

  • Inpatient and Outpatient Coding Support

    Staffing shortages, changes in workflows, seasonal fluctuations, and coding reimbursement updates all impact your organization’s financial bottom line. When faced with these issues, our clients turn to QualCode to provide expert coding support equipped to meet the needs of our complex healthcare environment.

  • HCC Medicare Advantage Coding Support

    Submitting an inaccurate diagnosis or a diagnosis resulting in the wrong hierarchical condition category (HCC) is a compliance risk, possibly leading to incorrect payment under the Medicare risk adjustment program. Our coding experts review the medical record documentation, assess the quality for appropriateness, and assign the ICD-9-CM diagnosis codes based on CMS guidelines.

  • RAC/Denial Management

    Contractors for federal and state agencies, such as Medicare’s Recovery Audit Contractor (RAC) are here to stay with Medicaid and other payers soon to follow. QualCode’s denial management expertise is designed to assist healthcare providers in preparing and responding to RAC requests for clinical information.

  • ICD-10-CM/PCS Coding Education and Training

    Make sure your staff is sufficiently trained in the new ICD-10-CM/PCS coding system. QualCode’s AHIMA-Approved ICD-10-CM/PCD Trainers are prepared to get your staff on board with the changes in the structure and characteristics of the I-10 coding system, coding conventions and guidelines, and new definitions of the terminology.

  • DRG Validation Audits

    Coding errors can lead to over or under payment of hospital inpatient claims. Our auditing professionals are skilled at assessing the quality of your data, identifying trends, and documentation issues that could be preventing your healthcare facility from achieving its maximum reimbursement potential. Note: All audits are followed up with an educational component.

  • Evaluation and Management Audits

    Chart audits for E/M services are done to determine if the documentation is compliant, the claim is coded correctly, and all charges are captured. Our chart audits measure the provider compliance with coding and documentation requirements for the E/M services, office procedures, modifier usage, and diagnosis code supporting medical necessity and surgical procedures.

    • Emergency Room
    • Clinics
    • Physician Practice
  • Professional Practice

    Benefit from QualCode’s experience of ensuring your documentation is clear, consistent and complete. We’ll make sure your coding is accurate and in compliance with federal and state contractors (RAC, MAC, ZPIC, MIC and HEAT). Acting as the mediator between the documentation and the claim, we will review and verify all documentation is present and supports what is being billed.

    • Documentation and Coding Audits
    • Superbill Review (for Correct CPT/HCPCS/ICD Assignment)
