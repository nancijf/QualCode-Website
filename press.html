Press & Media


Since 2000, QualCode, Inc., has helped hospitals, clinics and physician practices keep their cash flow steady. Our team of highly trained and credentialed coding and billing professionals are backed by more than 25 years of industry experience.

Being a HIPAA-compliant agency, we proudly support and uphold the code of ethics of the American Health Information Management Association (AHIMA). We maintain active memberships in several professional organizations, including the American Health Information Management Association (AHIMA), the Health Information Management Association of New York City (HIMANYC), the New York Health Information Management Association (NYHIMA), the Healthcare Financial Management Association (HFMA) and the American Academy of Professional Coders (AAPC).
