About Us


QualCode helps hospitals and professional practices of all sizes keep their cash flow steady. We provide skilled medical coding and billing personnel for temporary and permanent assignments, both full- and part-time. Careful screening and selection, coupled with continuous training, mean fewer coding and billing errors—and faster payment for services.

QualCode also offers audit and review services, as well as medical data abstraction. Seasoned, credentialed experts eliminate backlogs and other billing concerns. You focus on other priorities—from patient care and clinical research to other business operations—while we manage your revenue stream.

In addition, we share our expertise with client employees through education and training.

Any successful partnership requires honor and integrity. A HIPAA-compliant agency, we proudly support and uphold the code of ethics of the American Health Information Management Association (AHIMA).

Natalie Williams, MPA, RHIA
Founder and President


With over 20 years of healthcare reimbursement experience, Natalie knows how to help her clients’ navigate the complicated system of medical coding. As founder and president of QualCode, Inc., she selects and matches the right personnel to each assignment, enabling QualCode to fit seamlessly within client organizations.

Natalie has built a career on understanding medical financial services. Her extensive knowledge of CMS, both inpatient and outpatient prospective payment systems and vast experience in medical record review all contribute to QualCode’s success in helping clients receive maximum reimbursement.

Natalie is a Certified Professional Coder and maintains active memberships in several professional organizations, including the American Health Information Management Association (AHIMA), the New York Health Information Management Association (NYHIMA) and the American Association of Professional Coders (AAPC).

In addition to her credentials, Natalie holds a bachelor’s degree in health information management from Touro College in New York and a master’s from the Wagner School of Public Administration at New York University.
